Review: Gilmore Girls (TV show)

 

 

 

 

I interviewed my friend Musty Mustard about a recent episode of the TV show ‘The Gilmore Girls’.

 

The Incredible Sinking Lorelais (s4 e14)

 

According to Musty,  in this episode, Rory is at Yale, and Lorelai is at home. They call but keep missing each other. Then they cry, and they say this is all messed up. Lorelai is crying to Luke, and Rory is crying to Dean.

 

My friend Musty Mustard doesn’t recommend to watch this particular episode. Instead Musty recommends to watch an episode a few before this in the series. Musty recommends me to watch the episode where they break the town bells.

 

In the Clamor and the Clangor (s4 e11)

 

The baby knows the town bells schedule, so it cries in anticipation of the bells!
