A Friend of Mine Goes To Fighting Class

 

Early in the morning, at 6am sharp, a friend of mine, Musty Mustard, goes to fighting class.

 

Did you know that <a href="https://lemonmama.com/lemonmama/2025/2025%20February%20Blog%20Posts.html#0083">fighting class was invented by dogs</a>, as a sport to stay sharp and active?

 

Musty rolls and pushes, pulls and strains.

Musty wrestles and tubmles with a friend.

 

My friend Musty Mustard is going out in the community, getting stronger, and setting a good example, at Musty's fighting class.

 

They stand and push, they run and chase.

They bite and choke each other, but not too hard.

 

Musty and his fighting friends are good friends.
