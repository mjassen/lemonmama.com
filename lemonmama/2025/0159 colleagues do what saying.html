Colleagues Say What Doing And Do What Saying

 

I want to work with these folks:

 

- They say what they are doing

 

- They do what they are saying

 

- They aren't doing nothing

 

- They are doing valuable work

 

- They are omitting unimportant work

 

- The work we are speaking and doing, aligns with what is important

 

- The work we are doing makes an economic difference

 

A few of my colleagues in 2025 have done this!

I still need to work on it a lot.

In conclusion, I want to work with, and to be like, these professional folks. The ones who do what they are saying and say what they are doing and are doing important work.
