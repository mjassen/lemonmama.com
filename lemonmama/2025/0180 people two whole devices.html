...Choose both.

 

Ok so it took so long to get to this point.

 

Each person should have two fully active mobile devices.

 

Including kids and teenagers.

 

We need a permanent solid alternative available 24/7/365.

That is the dumphone.

That is freedom.

 

And we also need access to the mainstream apps and channels all the time.

That is the smartphone.

That is freedom too.

 

1.)

One is a fully activated connected mainstream dumbphone.

 

2.)

The other is a fully activated dumbphone (talk and text only, no app store nor play store, small screen, no touch screen, T9 style keypad)

 

Why?

 

The reason why is the person, i.e. every person on earth, needs the freedom and relief of taking just a dumbphone whenever they want while still having basic connectivity to friends and family.    

 

Why?

 

However the person also needs to have smartphone to fit in, and needs to have smartphone to communicate via digital channels with people too which is normal.

 

Because in 2025, ALL of the social companies and app and device companies, are ransoming all of their users.

 

So the conclusion is.

 

Give the person two devices. a smartphone AND a dumbphone.

 

It's 2025.

This is the best solution I have so far.

 

~Here is the background that led up to this >>

The step just a few months before this was, to get ONE extra shared dumbphone line, akin to a land line, that stays at home all the time for anybody at home who wants to borrow it or to use it.

This is another step toward individual freedom.

The shared phone is a channel that can allow trapped people to escape the ransom of the smartphone (app / device / service / media) ecosystem if they want.

^^ That was the background that led up to this. A step in the right direction. However it is shared still. This is America. American people should have individual freedom, including the choice to use, or not use, smartphone, all the time. I posit this will promote healthy free smart strong personal american development.

 

So going forward. One solution is that each person gets whole own dumbphone and whole own smartphone.

Do it. Dooooo It!

Because it is that important.

It's so important that it's worth the cost of two active lines and two devices available at all times to each person.

 

 

 

In conclusion, each person gets multiple fully active devices. It's a false dichotomy to have one-and-only-one device per person, so, to fix this, each person gets one whole own dumbphone AND whole own smartphone.
