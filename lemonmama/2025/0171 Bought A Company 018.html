How do other folks deal with this if they decide they want to become wealthy and then if they do start to have success towards becoming wealthy?

 

1.)

I reckon a lot of people just keep quiet and don't talk about money so as to not make waves.

 

2.)

I reckon a lot of people just give up, stop saving and stop investing, and stay poor, because they don't want to have to deal with their peers calling them greedy and calling them not-hard-working (no matter what degree of truth there).

 

3.)

I reckon other people have other ways of dealing with this.

I think a lot of people as they grow richer and richer, they turn to philanthropy, and try to give as much as they can back into the world, and in this way, they feel good that yes they are wealthy and also they feel good that they are sharing their wealth with others.

 

4.)

I also saw many stories where folks who became rich, then started teaching and coaching others, how to become rich too.

Including money authors and money bloggers and money podcasters.

So that way they spread the knowledge and spread the wealth.

 

In conclusion, I want to make my life better and invest to get richer, however I know I'll be called greedy by some people if I do. And so I'll have to deal with that. And so I'll have some options and choices mentioned here, such as keeping it secret, or quitting, or becoming a philanthropist, or becoming a money coach, etc., to deal with that as I go.
