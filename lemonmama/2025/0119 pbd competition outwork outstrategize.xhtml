#0119 - Competition， Suffering， Discomfort

 

 

 

The persona of Patrick Bet-David has said various things about competition.

 

One of these is really making me re-think the human paradigm, and my paradigm.

 

Paraphrased, the essence of the idea that I am learning as PBD has been talking about it over the years especially recently:

 

'we are all in a competition, there is no choice to not be in a competition, it is the human condition'

 

PBD Podcast Episode 379 (2024-Mar-12)

PBD Podcast Episode 380 (2024-Mar-14)

 

Later I was myself meditating further on this.

This is life.

This theme keeps appearing to me recently in other different quotes in my life:

Buddhism:           'Humans are put here on earth to suffer.'

Christianity:       'We are all born sinners.'

Sports Coach:              'Get comfortable being uncomfortable.'

 

 

But getting back to PBD quotes/ideas.

I recommend listening/watching the two above podcast episodes.

Because PBD and team recommends that to beat the competition one should strive to do some of the following things:

Outwork

Out improve

Outthink

Out strategize

Outlast

 

In conclusion, if you want to hear more on these refreshing life strategies, then these podcast episodes may be for you!
