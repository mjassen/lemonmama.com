Why I like GNU Gami (Jami.net) Messaging App

 

GNU Jami is an open source messaging and calling app.

 

https://jami.net/

 

Jami rocks because:

 

 

1.) The app doesn't spam you with ads nor notifications.

 

2.) The app is free.

 

3.) The app is cross platform.  (iOS, Android, Windows, Linux, Mac)

 

4.) Jami is private and secure.

 

5.) My data isn't stored on any server.

 

6.) No need to link any phone number or email address. Just username and password.   (public key is username, private key is password)

 

7.) I can have unlimited number of permanent accounts.

 

 

Ok but for some downsides. To be fair.

Downside: it is peer to peer app. Since my data isn't stored on a server, thus, i need to be online in order to send/receive messages.

 

In some ways, 2025 Jami messaging app, reminds me like of how AOL Messenger app was in 1995.

 

In conclusion, these are reasons to like GNU Jami messaging app.
