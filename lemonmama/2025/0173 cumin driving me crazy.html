This word is driving me crazy!!!

 

We always pronounced it like "KYOO-min".

 

But now I hear more people than not pronounce it like "KOO-min".

 

The word is: cumin

 

So next to go old reliable source to check pronunciation. ahdictionary.com.

 

https://ahdictionary.com/word/search.html?q=cumin

 

Downloaded the audio sound bit from there and they are pronouncing it like:

"KUH-min"

 

 

AAAARRGGGGHHHHH!

 

Three pronunciations for the same word!!!
