50 Co-Owners Of Each Company

 

 

 

 

 

 

Globally there is enough room to have 50 co-owners of each company.

 

I did the math.

 

We work in these tribes.

 

and each of the 50 people invest ₷¤10,000 Ye Olden Shells in the company.

 

(that is 10% of their salary for 10 years in a row roughly)

 

and then

 

once each person is bought in.

 

maybe they will get ₷¤1,500 Ye Olden Shells per year additional profit income cash flow.

 

That is in addition to their salary.

 

for being part owner.

 

 

nice.

 

this is conservative and realistic.

 

more liberal and realistic  is where not everyone invests.

 

only 25% of people invest. and so their returns are greater.

 

but the other folks are in the rat race.

 

 

 

In conclusion, on earth, each company could have 50 co-owners.
