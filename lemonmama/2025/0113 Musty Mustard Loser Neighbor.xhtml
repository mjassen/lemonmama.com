My Friend Musty Mustard has a Loser Neighbor

 

 

 

 

A friend of mine, Musty Mustard, has a screw up neighbor.

 

My friend Musty's neighbor is almost normal, but is a bit weird.

 

Musty doesn't quite trust the neighbor.

 

But Musty Mustard usually gives everyone a chance.

 

One day, Musty's neighbor invited Musty to tea and conversation.

 

Musty accepted.

 

It was ok, but a  little odd.

 

A few months later, my friend Musty Mustard again was invited to tea by Musty's neighbor.

 

Musty accepted on the condition that they will meet at the park instead of the cafe, because Musty thought to go for a walk and get some exercise while they conversed.

 

It was ok, but Musty's friend was a bit of a screw up, they talked but didn't seem to connect very well.

 

Musty's friend wasn't trying to sell anything nor to buy anything, just wanted to talk and be mildly annoying.

 

My friend Musty Mustard told me, if Musty's neighbor invites Musty out again, then Musty will probably postpone another meeting until year-after-next. (to give the neighbor time to mature up a bit more)

 

In conclusion, a friend of mine, Musty Mustard, has a loser neighbor.
