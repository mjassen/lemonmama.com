Morgan note: On the podcast technology aspect, I noticed specifically, that each guest/host had their own separate tabletop microphone on the table in front of them pointed at them (about 12" from their mouth or 18" at most). No one was wearing earphones. They were all 3x attendees seated around the same table in the same room.

Nice, it sounded pretty good while still not needing headphones nor head mics. And compared to say JRE podcast or PBD podcast, which iirc both use headphones, and which both ask the guests to talk directly into a boom mic.

But so I do hear this inside lowell episode sounds just a bit more tinny compared to JRE and PBD, However, this episode sound quality isn't bad, sound quality not bad at all; I can hear all the content.

27m. mark.

Morgan note: At/around/before the 27m. mark, yes they did talk for a long time about battery fires topics. lol. And they admit that on the podcast and joke about it. Nice, I like this podcast episode because they are talking on-topic and yet are keeping it light tone too.
