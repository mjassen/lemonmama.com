Let me tell you a story about my friend Musty Mustard.

 

I was walking in town.

My friend Musty Mustard was walking Musty’s dog in town.

 

Musty had just returned from a business trip.

I was going out for a walk in town, and then for a drive.

Musty asked me what I had in my bag.

 

I just started talking about the weather because I didn’t want to say what was in my bag.

Because I keep secret stuff in my bag sometimes.

And it’s good to keep good boundaries with friends sometimes.

 

“Good fences make good neighbors” they say.

 

In conclusion, sometimes I keep secret stuff in my bag, and also good fences make good neighbors.
