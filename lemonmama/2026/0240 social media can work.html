Social Media Can Work




Here is how social media can work:


1.) Person A keeps a short offline list of friends' names.
2.) Next to each name is a web addresses.
3.) Friend is say Person B.
4.) That web address is where Person B posts stuff.
5.) Each month one time, Person A clicks through the links and reads what friends posted.
