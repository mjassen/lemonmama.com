1.)

My friend Musty Mustard.

Is very cool and very calculating.

But still humble and chill.

 

2.)

My friend Musty, acts friendly.

Until Musty suddenly and smoothly puts me on the hook.

To try to get me to do something.

 

3.)

When this happens, Musty just decides Musty wants me to do something.

And decides that smooth subtle manipulation, bordering on gaslighting, is a good way to get me to do it.

And Musty doesn’t waste time considering whether asking me directly or telling me directly may be a more effective route.

Instead Musty proceeds to smoothly and gently put me on the hook and pressure and manipulate me into doing that thing.

 

4.)

And similarly.

My friend musty mustard.

When we work together on a project.

Musty speaks at lengths, verbiages that downplay the difficulty of a project and a task.

Verbiages that pump up the ease of a project and task.

 

5.)

And so in this way.

My friend Musty Mustard is setting me up, like a fall guy is set up.

Musty is setting me up claiming a project is easier than it is.

So that I will be on the hook to do the project.

On the hook to finish the project.

Be on my heels while starting the project.

Be on my heels while doing the project.

 

6.)

How did Musty get this way?

Did someone do this to Musty and now Musty is doing this to others?

Did Musty think of this on-the-hook paradigm?

I may never know.

 

7.)

However I know this.

Musty is still fairly humble and genuine and chill throughout.

Musty seems to live a modest lifestyle.

And yet still seems hold me, accountable to perform work on a project.

 

8.)

Amazing.

 

9.)

In summary, my friend Musty Mustard is cool and calculating while still remarkably humble and chill.
