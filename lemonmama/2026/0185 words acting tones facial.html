Subconsciously or consciously.

3.)

Considering further, one can try to steamroll another person, using either one of these previous two methods.

Subconsciously or consciously.

This can get one far in life.

 

4.)

Another paradigm is to only act according to one's emotions how one feels. That is for facial expression and for tone of voice.

Subconsciously or consciously.

This can get one far in life.

 

5.)

Another paradigm is to leverage facial expressions and body language and tone of voice consciously in order to do what you want to do.

Consciously.

This can get one far in life.

 

6.)

Another paradigm is to start off using one paradigm (like one of the five above, or else another), but then instantly switch to using another paradigm, as convenient for one. In other words to steamroll someone or win an argument or win a sale.

Note this may work a lot, however, some may say it is kind of insane. This method may lose friends in some cases. However some may say this is kind of brilliant. And some may admire this method. People in a position of positional/rank power or physically stronger people may do this more successfully if they use physical/positional power to back it up.

 

7.)

More people involved the dynamics change by multiples or magnitudes.

Person A and B both use conscious acting to express their wants. This is great. They both use all of the following to express themselves to each other, and they have a rich easy conversation:

(words + vocal tones + body language + facial expressions)

Call ^these the '5 rich communications'. (see below)

 

8.)

More people, more dynamic multiples.

Person A uses 5 Rich Communications but Person B only uses (words) (non consciously toned)

If either one recognizes which paradigms the other is using, and matches them, then it's fine, they can get along fine.

 

9.)

However if either party Person A or Person B.

Let's say Person A has a much broader and deeper understanding of conscious and unconscious usage of multiple human communication paradigms, and Person A also is pushing Person A’s agenda in a steamrolling fashion, and let's say Person A also further has and leverages positional and/or physical strength power, then, it can be a very hugely and sadly one-sided communication.

 

 

Moving on.

From 1-9 above, a main point is that there are a broad rich spectrum of paradigms to use for human communication.

And they all cross over and intertwine.

So we may call these the '5 Rich Communications'.

Made that up, because there are 5 and they are rich.

In short:

 

a.  (non consciously toned words + consciously toned words) is one

b.  (vocal tones + body language + facial expressions) are four more multipliers of magnitude.

c.  (Conscious + subconscious) multiplies the possibilities by an order of magnitude.

d.  (leveraging conscious + not-leveraging conscious) multiplies it by magnitude.

e.  (switching the paradigm midway instantly   +    not-switching-the-paradigm-miway-instantly)  multiplies it by an order of magnitude..

 

So now we have (2⁶ = 128) different paradigm-combinations. just discussed here.

 

And this is only a bit of what humans are capable.

 

Only one paradigm is actually really needed to get basic stuff done.

We're not talking the actual content here, just the communication paradigm being used.

 

Not the motives or context etc.

 

And so there are so many intertwined levels.
