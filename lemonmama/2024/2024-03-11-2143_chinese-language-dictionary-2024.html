This dictionary I just used pydict/xdict as a base.

Then I did Add in some other trusted dictionary sources too.

So most words are in traditional chinese from Taiwan and/or Hong Kong.

Which is one of the main ideas here, the integrity of a Taiwan and/or Hong Kong Chinese dictionary.

And then I added some dozens of my own words too.

Note: I have had this up live for a few years now, but not sure how much longer I will keep it live. Especially as I pay for the hosting and pretty sure I'm the only one using this. 
But for now (2024-03-11) it's live here:
