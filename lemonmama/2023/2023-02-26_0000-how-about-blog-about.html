How About Blogging About This Stuff

How about blog about:

- what I actually am literally doing

- what I am actually spending my money on literally

- what I actually know about from first-hand experience because I did it before literally

- stuff I know to be true from common sense and/or intuition/gut.

- what products + services I am actually using regularly

- total bullshit fake stuff that I 100% make up.

In conclusion, how about blogging about the above stuff?
