Apparently the full text of the book "The Richest Man in Babylon" by George Clason is available on archive.org.

I think this may be possible because the book was originally published in 1926 and so the 95-year public domain license has taken effect now that it's 2023.

In any case here is a link to the book scan and text and pdf etc posted now in 2023 on archive.org:

This is the html page where folks can choose their flavor (pdf or image or scanned-txt) here:

Direct link to the pdf scan of the entire book:
