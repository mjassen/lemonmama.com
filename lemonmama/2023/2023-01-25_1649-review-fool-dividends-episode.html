They say when interest rates are low in the ecosystem, then people, investors, tend to pile onto solid dividend stocks instead, and so the stock price goes high relatively.
And so now a dividend stock that would have had a percentage yield of maybe 5% at when the stock price was at a lower price, that same stock is now having a dividend percentage yield of maybe 1% or 2% because the price of the stock is so high and the price is a factor of the yield.
